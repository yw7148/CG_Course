Information

This is random box generator with mouse rotate & zoom

User Guide

1. Select a Texture you prefer

2. Generate a box at random space.

3. Find the box by rotating and zooming with your mouse.

4. You can also move the camera back('s') and front('w') with your keyboard.

5. You can double-click to reset.

More Infos

1. You can remove the center box with radio button.

2. You can generate more than 1 box.

3. you can limit box generation range.

4. mouse drag to rotate & mouse scroll to zoom
